// There are five elements in Arknights: Endfield. Physical stands apart — it builds Vulnerable stacks and bypasses the Arts system entirely. The other four (Heat, Electric, Cryo, Nature) apply Arts Inflictions that stack up to 4 times before triggering an Arts Burst. When two different Arts elements meet on the same enemy, an Arts Reaction fires.

Physical
STATUS — Vulnerable

Applies Vulnerable stacks instead of Arts Inflictions. At 4 stacks, Physical Status skills trigger powerful effects — Crush for burst, Lift for crowd-control, Knock Down to extend combos. Physical feeds the Shatter bonus reaction when paired with Cryo's Solidification.

Heat
REACTION — Combustion

Applies Heat Infliction and triggers Combustion when mixing elements. Combustion detonates on the enemy and applies a strong damage-over-time effect — excellent for burning down high-HP targets over time. Heat teams typically spike hardest against singular dangerous enemies.

Electric
REACTION — Electrification

Applies Electric Infliction. Electrification increases Arts DMG taken by the target from all sources for its duration — meaning every hit from every element benefits. Considered the strongest all-purpose reaction for Arts-heavy teams.

Cryo
REACTION — Solidification

Applies Cryo Infliction. Solidification freezes enemies in place — and a frozen enemy hit with Physical damage triggers Shatter, dealing a large burst. Strong against single targets. Cryo teams reward precise burst timing once Solidification is active.

Nature
REACTION — Corrosion

Applies Nature Infliction. Corrosion reduces the enemy's elemental resistance over time — the debuff grows stronger the longer it is active. Best reaction against tanky, high-defense enemies and bosses. Pairs extremely well with any Arts DPS.

// Arts Reactions fire when a second Arts element is applied to an enemy that already has a different Arts Infliction active. The triggering element is what determines the reaction — the first element applied doesn't matter. Master this and you control every fight.

HEAT
Combustion
TRIGGER — Apply Heat to an enemy with another active Infliction

Detonates for initial Arts damage then applies a Damage-over-Time debuff that deals ATK-scaled damage every second. The explosion and DoT strength scale with how many Infliction stacks were on the target. Best for sustained damage against a single hard target.

CRYO
Solidification
TRIGGER — Apply Cryo to an enemy with another active Infliction

Freezes the enemy in place, preventing movement and actions. Duration and initial damage both scale with prior Infliction stacks. Enemies who cannot be frozen still take the Cryo damage. Solidified enemies become set up for the Shatter bonus reaction.

ELECTRIC
Electrification
TRIGGER — Apply Electric to an enemy with another active Infliction

Deals initial Arts damage and applies a debuff that increases the target's Arts DMG taken from all sources. Potency and duration both scale with Infliction stacks at the time of trigger. Widely regarded as the strongest general-purpose reaction for elemental teams.

NATURE
Corrosion
TRIGGER — Apply Nature to an enemy with another active Infliction

Deals Nature damage and applies the Corrosion debuff, which reduces enemy elemental resistance over time. The resistance reduction grows increasingly potent before capping near expiration. Best against high-defense enemies and bosses who stay in the fight.

BONUS
Shatter
TRIGGER — Apply Physical Status / Vulnerable to a Solidified enemy

A bonus reaction that fires when a Solidified (frozen) enemy is hit with a Physical Status skill. Deals a massive burst of Physical damage — one of the highest single-hit damage numbers in the game. Shatter damage scales with both ATK and the prior Solidification stacks.

CONCEPT — Arts Burst
Same Element

Applying the same element repeatedly builds Infliction stacks up to 4. Each new stack triggers an Arts Burst — an immediate bonus hit of that element's damage. Running a mono-element team maximizes Burst uptime since every attack contributes to the same stack.

CONCEPT — Arts Reaction
Mixed Elements

Applying a second, different element to an enemy that already has active Inflictions consumes those stacks and fires an Arts Reaction. Mixed-element teams trade consistent Burst damage for powerful situational reactions — particularly Electrification or Corrosion.

Once the loop is clear, everything else clicks.

Arts

The game's term for elemental damage and mechanics. Arts DMG refers to damage from Heat, Electric, Cryo, or Nature elements.

Arts Infliction

A debuff stacked on enemies when elemental Arts damage is dealt. Stacks up to 4 times. Triggers Arts Burst on each new stack. Consumed when a different element triggers a Reaction.

Arts Burst

Fires automatically when a new Infliction stack of the same element is applied. Deals bonus elemental damage scaled to the operator's ATK. Maximized by running mono-element teams.

Arts Reaction

Triggered when a second, different Arts element is applied to an enemy with active Inflictions. Consumes stacks and fires the reaction corresponding to the second element used.

Vulnerable

The Physical equivalent of Arts Infliction. Stacks to 4 via Lifts, knockdowns, and Physical skills. Physical Status skills spend Vulnerable for effects like Crush (burst), Lift, or Knock Down.

SP

Skill Points — the resource fueling Combo Skills and Ultimates. Generated by basic attacks and specific Vanguard abilities. SP management is a core pillar of efficient team rotation.

Rotation

The ordered sequence in which you activate operator abilities to maximize reaction uptime and burst windows. A clean rotation is more valuable than raw stat increases.

Solidification

Cryo's Arts Reaction. Freezes susceptible enemies in place and enables the Shatter bonus reaction when followed by Physical Status. Duration and damage scale with prior Infliction stacks.

Shatter

A bonus Physical reaction that fires when a Solidified enemy is hit with Physical Status. Deals a large ATK-scaled burst of Physical damage and breaks the Solidification status.

Corrosion

Nature's Arts Reaction. Reduces enemy elemental resistance over time. The resistance reduction grows stronger near the end of its duration, making it exceptional in prolonged encounters.

Electrification

Electric's Arts Reaction. Increases Arts DMG taken by the enemy from all sources — not just Electric. Considered the strongest all-purpose amplification reaction in the current meta.

Combustion

Heat's Arts Reaction. Detonates the target and applies a DoT debuff that deals ATK-scaled damage every second. Best on high-HP targets where the sustained damage has time to accumulate.
