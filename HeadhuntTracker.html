Data Notice
⚠
Pull history is limited to the last 90 days. The Endfield API only returns records within a 90-day rolling window, regardless of your total playtime. Pulls older than 90 days are permanently excluded from this tracker. Pity counters and statistics shown here reflect only visible history — if your last 6★ was pulled more than 90 days ago, your displayed pity count may be understated.
