ℹ Pull Log Tips ▼
  • Use the banner tabs to switch between Special, Standard, Beginner, and other banners.
  • The @N number next to each 6★ or 5★ is the pity count — pulls since the last hit of that rarity.
  • Red pity numbers (6★ ≥50, 5★ ≥8) mean you were in or near soft pity.
  • Use the filter buttons to isolate pulls by rarity.
  • Records only go back 90 days — pity may display lower than real value if your last 6★ was before that window.

Detailed Statistics
ℹ Reading the Statistics ▼
  • Total Headhunts counts all pulls across every banner within the 90-day window.
  • Oroberyl Spent is estimated at 500 per pull.
  • Spark Progress shows your position in the current 120-pull spark cycle.
  • Lucky 6★ Pulls counts 6★ operators received in 40 or fewer pulls.
  • The 6★ Pull History lists every 6★ with pity count. Green = lucky (≤15), red = soft/hard pity (≥65).
ℹ Per-Banner Breakdown Tips ▼
  • Each card represents one banner pool. Special Headhunting (purple) is where limited operators appear.
  • Avg 6★ Pity is the mean pulls-per-6★ — lower is luckier.
  • Standard banner (blue) has no rate-up. Beginner (gold) guarantees a 6★ within 20 pulls.
