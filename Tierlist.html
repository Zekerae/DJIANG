Tier List — Introduction

Arknights: Endfield is a game where team building matters immensely, but at the same time, one where many units are usable only within a single element or team archetype. As a result, there are two tier lists: a General Tier List (this page) and a Team Tier List (accessible via the tab above).

Our general tier list ranks all characters within categories according to their role and contribution in teams, as well as how impactful they are to the game's meta. For the story and the lower difficulties of permanent game modes, there is no need to be concerned with ratings. Players should be able to comfortably clear such content so long as a team is built and played correctly.

Instead, the tier list criteria are based on the current hardest content in the game — Umbral Monument. As new or harder content is released, the tier list and its criteria will be updated accordingly.

In Endfield, many characters possess elements of multiple categories. To avoid cluttering the tier list, each character is placed in a single category — whichever they're strongest in.

  • Damage — Characters whose main purpose is to deal damage, working as hypercarries or driving a team's damage output.
  • Enabler — Characters whose main purpose is to assist others beyond buffs: Inflictions, SP generation, stagger build, Ultimate Energy, and more.
  • Buffer — Characters whose main purpose is to provide buffs or debuffs that multiply the overall damage output of the squad.

Special Tags

  • [Expert] — Characters with this tag have an optimal playstyle that must be mastered. Without the necessary skills, performance may drop by at least one tier.
  • [Watchlist ▲/▼] — Characters marked as highly likely to move up or down a tier in a future update.

The general criteria are structured around Umbral Monument. Assessment assumes:

  • Operators at Level 90, Skills at max Rank, all Talents fully levelled.
  • Optimal Gear/Sets equipped.
  • P0 for 6★ and 5★ Operators; P5 for 4★ and Endministrator.
  • Best-in-slot weapon assumed (P0 6★ weapon; P5 5★/4★/3★ weapon) with optimal essences.
  • All Operators start at zero Ultimate Energy; no pre-casting before combat.
  • Usables (food, Tacticals) are not included. Evaluations are done around optimal team compositions.

Characters are compared within their role, not between roles. If a category has no Operators who perform at a given level, some tiers for that category may be empty.

Team Tier List — Introduction

The team tier list ranks the performance of full team compositions relative to one another, factoring in their peak potential in endgame content. For story and lower difficulties, there is no need to be concerned with ratings.

The current hardest content, Umbral Monument, is the mode these ratings are built around. Team placements are determined by their overall efficacy across encounters, with higher teams being more capable of consistently clearing faster.

The characters listed aren't the only viable compositions, but are the ones capable of the highest damage output. Lower-tier units and Survivalists are less likely to appear. All variations of a team's flex slots are listed where applicable.

The team tier list aims to cover teams from two angles:

  • Ensuring all meta-relevant teams are ranked.
  • Including as many viable team archetypes as possible to encompass as much of the cast as possible.

When assessing the power of a team for tier placement, only the best character combinations are considered. For character-specific criteria, refer to the General Tier List.
